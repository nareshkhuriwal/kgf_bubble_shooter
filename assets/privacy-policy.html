Privacy Policy

Bubble Kingdom  ·  Khuriwalgroup

Effective Date: May 15, 2025

Welcome to Bubble Kingdom, a mobile puzzle game developed and published by Khuriwalgroup. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

✦  Short version: Bubble Kingdom does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All game progress is stored locally on your device only.

🔍 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Bubble Kingdom does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The game does not collect or transmit:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone, address)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, IP address)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports sent to remote servers
  • Camera, microphone, or contact data
💾 2. Local Data Storage

To provide a seamless gameplay experience, Bubble Kingdom saves certain game data locally on your device using the device's built-in storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Locally stored data includes:

  • Level progress and star/crown ratings
  • High score and total glory points
  • Coin balance
  • Daily reward streak and last claim date
  • Number of levels unlocked
✦  You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the application.
🔐 3. Device Permissions

Bubble Kingdom requests the following Android permissions solely to support gameplay features:

  • INTERNET — Required by the Android/Expo framework. The game does not use the internet connection to transmit or receive any user data.
  • VIBRATE — Used for haptic feedback during gameplay (bubble pops, combos). No data is collected through this permission.
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS — Allows the game to manage audio volume and sound effects. No data is collected through this permission.
🔗 4. Third-Party Services

Bubble Kingdom does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms, social media SDKs, or data brokers. We do not use services such as:

  • Google AdMob or any ad network
  • Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar
  • Facebook SDK or social login
  • Crash reporting services (e.g. Sentry, Crashlytics)
  • Attribution or marketing analytics tools

The application is built using the React Native and Expo frameworks. These frameworks may include standard network libraries, but no data collection is configured or enabled within this application.

👶 5. Children's Privacy

Bubble Kingdom is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal data from any user, the game is safe for children to play. We comply with applicable children's privacy laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's use of this application, please contact us at the address below. Since no data is collected, there is nothing to review or delete beyond uninstalling the app.

🛡️ 6. Data Security

Because Bubble Kingdom does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being intercepted, leaked, or misused through our services. All game save data remains exclusively on your device, protected by your device's own security mechanisms.

We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated and using a screen lock to protect the data stored on your device.

📋 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date at the top. If we ever change our data practices in a material way, we will provide notice through the Google Play Store listing.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the game after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
